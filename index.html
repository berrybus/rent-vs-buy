Rent vs Buy Calculator

Methodology and Assumptions
  • Don't look too closely at the total NW, look more at the difference
  • We assume that you have enough money to afford either the renting or buying option, so we look at the opportunity cost for each option
  • If the monthly payment of rent is higher than buy, the difference goes in the investment bucket for buying and vice versa
  • American tax savings
  • Can always deduct SALT cap
  • I made this calculator for personal use, but I hope you can get some use out of it too. I found the existing calculators either too simple or too black-box, so if you're ever wondering about the exact calculations, you can just check out the source code.
  • If you see any issues, email me at nancy@redbeancat.com or create an issue in the repo
